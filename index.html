Good to know

Frequently asked

Is IV therapy safe?

Every treatment is administered by a licensed nurse or paramedic after a short health screening. We use single-use, sterile supplies and clinician-prepared formulas.

How long does a session take?

Most drips run about 45 minutes from setup to finish. NAD+ infusions are administered slowly and take longer — we'll let you know when you book.

Do you really come to me?

Yes. We bring everything to your home, office, hotel or gym across greater Miami, or you can visit our studio in Coconut Grove.

Can I pause my membership?

Absolutely. There are no contracts — pause or cancel anytime from your account, and unused credits roll over for 60 days.

Longevity & Cellular

NAD+ Renewal

A cellular reset for clarity, energy, and longevity.

What it does

Why people book NAD+ Renewal.

NAD+ Renewal works at the cellular level. NAD+ is a coenzyme central to how your cells produce energy and repair themselves, and levels naturally decline with age and stress. This infusion is our deepest reset — for clarity, sustained energy, and long-term resilience.

Because NAD+ is delivered slowly for comfort, plan for 90 minutes or more. It's a favorite for high performers recovering from burnout and anyone investing in longevity.

What's inside

NAD+ (250–500 mg)

The coenzyme at the center of cellular energy and repair.

Glutathione

Antioxidant support to complement the reset.

B-Complex

Amino Support

Aids cellular recovery and balance.

Electrolyte Fluids

Our Story

Built by the people who carry the bags and start the lines.

Elev8 was born from the field and the bedside — where two clinicians saw, every day, how much faster people feel better with the right fluids and nutrients. So they brought that care out of the clinic and to your door.

The mission

Hospital-grade. Gym-floor ready.

We believe recovery shouldn't require a waiting room. Whether you're an athlete chasing a PR, a parent running on four hours of sleep, or a traveler stepping off a red-eye, your body responds to the same thing: smart hydration, delivered well.

Elev8 pairs hospital-grade standards with concierge convenience. Every formula is single-dose and clinician-prepared, every visit starts with a health screen, and every drip is administered by a licensed professional. The result is care you can actually feel — usually the same day.
